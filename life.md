## Life

Outside of research, I find a lot of myself in music, stories, and places. I love listening to ’90s alternative rock, I’m a huge FC Barcelona fan, and I never get tired of classic TV dramas and sitcoms. 

1994 is my favourite year of all time. It is a year I return to without planning to. Some of my favourite rock albums and movies come from that time. Albums like Soundgarden’s Superunknown, Weezer’s Blue Album, and Oasis’s Definitely Maybe still feel relevant every time I listen to them. The same goes for films like The Shawshank Redemption, Forrest Gump, and The Lion King.
